What is FlashSwap ?

FlashSwap is a DeFi protocol that enables users to take on lend and borrow redeemable synthetic assets to make leveraged trades on Decentralized Exchanges such as Uniswap.

What is a Flash Loan ?

Instead of guaranteeing repayment with collateral, Flash Loans simply rely on the timing of the loanâ€™s repayment. As long as the loan is used and paid back in full within the same block it was issued, it is approved. On the other hand, if the loan is not paid back within the same block, the entire transaction fails..

What is SWP ?

SWP is a governance and fee accrution token, while lenders and liquidity providers earn fees in the used tokens, an extra SWP fees is given to the lenders to optimize utilisation rate. The protool updates are also managed trough a Decentralized Autonomous Organization where SWP is burned to vote on decisions and upgrades.
