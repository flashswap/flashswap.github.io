F.A.Q

What is FlashSwap ?

FlashSwap is a DeFi community driven lending protocol that enables users to lend and borrow a large number of cryptocurrencies using both stable and variable interest rates. With the in-built Flash-Loans anyone can margin trade on Uniswap, 1.inch... using 0 collateral.

When will the protocol be public ?

To make sure we don't put user funds at risk, the protocol is currently being audited. The audit is expected to be finished by end of August, protocol UI release will follow right after. You can check the contracts yourself on our Github.

What is a Flash Loan ?

Instead of guaranteeing repayment with collateral, flash loans use on-chain transactions to insure of the loan’s repayment. As long as the loan is used and paid back in full within the same block it was issued, it is approved. On the other hand, if the loan is not paid back within the same block, the entire trade fails.

What are fTokens ?

fTokens are simply interest accrution tokens with a mint and burn mechanism on deposits and redeemptions. Each fToken is pegged 1:1 its underlying asset deposited in Flash Pools. fTokens, such as fDai, can be freely stored, transferred, and traded. While the underlying asset is loaned out to borrowers, fTokens are rewarded interest in real time, and can be redeemed at any time.

What is FSWP ?

FSWP is limited supply token with a principal goal of interlocking governance and fee accrution towards liquidity providers. As a pool provider you earn rewards in the assets you deposit and a dynamic daily reward in FSWP shares, as a borrower you get discounted fees.

What are the risks of flash loans?

Flash loans can only be used in the same block their being repaid at, meaning that they're atomic. If the loans is paid back in the same block the transaction succeeds otherwise it fails.
